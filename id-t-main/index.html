WELKOM BIJ ID&T

Sustainable festivals


ID&T started in 1992 with a grassroot party, created by electronic dance music devotees who yearned for a new experience. We are famous for launching ground breaking concepts, such as Sensation, which made the world dance in white and are co-founder of Tomorrowland, which is the biggest festival in the world.

First to do it


We were the first to bring dance music to the masses, and our example has been leading the industry ever since. Our passion and devotion to the overall festival experience is what continues to keep us at the forefront of the electronic music world. We aim engage, amaze and captivate audiences through ever-evolving ideas, creating goosebumps all the way. Three decades of building our company have resulted in a tight execution machine, recognized worldwide for its leadership and quality in logistics and show.
